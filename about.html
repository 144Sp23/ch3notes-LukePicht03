Common Excercises

The following are common excercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of theri daily excersie routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strenth and undurance. Begin in a standing position drop into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward agin quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strenth. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forarms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain climbers
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place you hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can (as though you are climbing a mountain).
